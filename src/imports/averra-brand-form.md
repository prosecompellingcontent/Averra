AVERRA Brand Alignment Form

This takes 3–5 minutes.
Clarity now creates stronger visuals later.

1. Business Information

Full Name
Business Name
Instagram Handle
Website (if applicable)

2. What services are you currently offering?

Short answer.

3. Where would you say your business is right now?

• Building consistency
• Refining and raising standards
• Expanding and scaling
• Managing a team or multi-chair space

4. What feels slightly misaligned in your brand right now?

• Inconsistent content
• My pricing doesn’t feel fully supported
• My presence doesn’t reflect my level
• I’m growing and my brand hasn’t caught up
• I’m not sure, I just know it needs work

5. How do you want your brand to be perceived?

Short answer.
(Example: Established. Premium. Clean. Soft luxury. Bold. Editorial.)

6. Who is your ideal client right now?

Short answer.
Age range, vibe, spending level, etc.

7. Are you planning any of the following in the next 6–12 months?

• Raising prices
• Launching a new service
• Adding retail
• Hiring or expanding a team
• Repositioning in your market
• None of the above

8. When it comes to AI-generated brand visuals, where do you stand?

• Excited and ready
• Open but curious
• Slightly skeptical but willing
• I care more about the result than the tool

(This keeps normalizing the conversation without making it a big deal.)

9. Is there anything urgent we should know?

Optional.
Launch dates, rebrands, deadlines, etc.
